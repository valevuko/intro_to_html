Kopacki rit

Nature park

Story of the Park

Kopacki rit – a wetland home to thousands of species. A paradise for birds, and shelter to hundreds of bird species. A natural triangle between the rivers Danube and Drava, and the largest internal delta of the Danube. This area is one of the largest European wetlands, and it never looks the same, with every new visit bringing a new insight into nature. Kopački rit is a mystical place where one returns to the unhindered fullness of life.

Explore the park:

Bicycling

Provides a genuine cycling experience of Baranja. All three trails are part of international Cycling along the Pannonian Peace Trail, Danube Cycle Route or Baranja Greenways Eco Trail cycling routes, and they are open throughout the year. They are almost completely asphalted, and it is also possible to rent a bike. These marked and relaxing trails also provide access to high-quality accommodation, restaurants and natural and cultural points of interest.

Boat trips and rentals

The boat waiting at the Veliki Sakadaš pier takes visitors into the adventure of exploring the floodplains of the Danube. Starting from the lake of Sakadaš, the boat passes through the Čonakut channel to Hordovanj. Kopačko jezero is the largest lake, with large colonies of various bird species such as wild geese, ducks, cormorants, herons and egrets, terns, gulls, and the rulers of Kopački rit forests – white-tailed eagles
